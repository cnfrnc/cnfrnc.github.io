Placeholder page for Sparr's idea to host a small conference, probably in San Francisco, for code golf and esoteric programming languages. Inspired by the 2016 Roguelike Celebration conference.

Who might present at this conference?

  • Designers of esoteric languages
  • Developers of language interpreters
  • Writers/hosts of code golf and similar challenges
  • Admins/mods of esolang and codegolf sites/forums/wikis

What sort of events might be scheduled at this conference?

  • Presentations
  • Tutorials and other education on specific esolangs
  • Design and development of new esolangs
  • Contests
    • code golf
    • use of new/uncommon/multiple esolangs
    • other coding challenges
